THREAD COMMUNICATION :  In a Multithreaded environment thread often need to communicate and
                        coordinate with each other to accomplish a task.

- Without proper communication mechanism , threads might end  up in inefficient busy-waiting
    states, leading to wastage of CPU resources and potential deadlocks.


FOR COMMUNICATION A THREADS USED THIS METHOD:

wait()
notify()
notifyAll()
